🩺 How to Check Your Kidney Health on KidneySync

Follow these simple steps:

◆
Step 1:
Go to the KidneySync website.
◆
Step 2:
Click the "Check Now" button.
◆
Step 3:
Select Option 1 from the menu.
◆
Step 4:
Upload the following reports:
🧪 Blood Urea Test
🩸 Blood Glucose Test
💧 Urine Analysis Test

Wait a few moments while the AI OCR system processes your reports.

◆
Step 5:
Type in your Blood Pressure (mmHg) reading.
◆
Step 6:
Click the "Analyze" button to view your personalized kidney health results.
